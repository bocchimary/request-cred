Services

The Office of the Registrar of PTS College and Advanced Studies offers the following:

Request

Easily request academic credentials such as transcripts, certifications, and TOR online.

Clearance

Apply for your school clearance online without the need to visit every department manually.

Graduation

Submit your graduation application online and stay updated on your graduation status.

Guidelines:

How to Request Credentials

  1. Sign up as either a student or alumni.
  2. Check your email for the verification code sent by the system.
  3. Log in and click "Request" on the sidebar.
  4. Fill out the request form and click Submit.
  5. Wait for a confirmation email with your appointment details to claim your requested document.
